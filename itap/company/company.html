Established in 1989, ITAP was envisioned to cater to the burgeoning need of agro products in the international market. Excellence in enterprise was the propelling vision that inspired the birth of our parent company, 85 years ago at Tuticorin, a booming port city located in the strategic southern tip of peninsular India.

ITAP, as a grower-exporter with systematic & streamlined production and fundamentally sound farm operations, can undertake a growing, processing and supply program round the year against any specific requirement to meet any international demand.

Our products like gherkins (in natural vinegar/acetic acid/brine), pickled onions (in brine /acetic acid), baby corn and pepper (chillies) are a big success in various international markets. USA, Canada, Australlia, New Zealand, France, Spain, Netherlands, Belgium, UK, Italy, Greece, Argentina, Brazil, Hungary, Russia, Korea, and Japan are but a few of the countries wherein our esteemed clientele vouch for our supreme quality products. The products are available in bulk barrels and also in Cans and Bottle Jars

ITAP has over the years won many recognitions and awards for both quality and quantity. We have won for the past 4 years the APEDA Export award - Certificate of Merit - awarded by the Agricultural and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority, Ministry of Commerce, Government of India, for outstanding contribution to promotion of agricultural and processed food products.
